Itinerary

One Day - Mysuru Sight Seeings One Day

  • Day 1: Arrive at Mysore / Sightseeing

    One day Mysore sightseeing tour in private car sightseeing covers the best of Mysore city tourist attractions including places of interests in Srirangapatana and also Brindavan Gardens. Mysore city tour will cover best of all in a day.

    Chamundi Hills - Mysore Zoo - Mysore Palace Museum - Jaganmohana Palace - St.Philomena's Church - Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple - Nimishamba Temple - Gumbuz / Ganjam - Tippu's Summer Palace - Brindavan Gardens.

    Itinerary for One Day Mysore Local Sightseeing Cab Package

    Start Mysore city sightseeing tour. Visit Chamundi Hills to pay your obeisance to Goddess Chamundeshwari. On the way back to city you will see Statue of Mahishasura and Nandi and reach Mysore Zoo Garden and later visit Jaganmohana Palace Art Gallery. After lunch you will visit Mysore Palace and St. Philomena's Church. Later drive to Srirangapatna were you will visit Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple, Tippu's Summer Palace, Srirangapatna Fort and Gumbuz. And after you will be going to visit Brindavan Gardens, a show garden with fountains and world famous musical fountain.

Two Day - Mysuru Tour from Mysuru

  • Day 1: Arrive at Mysore / Sightseeing

    Meet on arrival at Mysore Railway Station / Bus Stand. Start Mysore sightseeing, visit Chamundi Hills to pay your obeisance to Goddess Chamundeshwari. On the way back to city you will see Statue of Mahishasura and Nandi. Pay a visit to Lalith Mahal Palace Hotel. And later visit Jaganmohan Palace Art Gallery. After lunch you will visit Mysore Palace, one of the largest palaces in the country and the beautiful St.Philomena's Church - the most majestic churches in India. Later drive to Brindavan Gardens a show garden that has botanical park with fountains as well as a musical fountain. Overnight stay at the hotel.

  • Day 2: Mysore Sightseeing / Departure

    After breakfast, visit and the Mysore Zoo, one of the oldest and well maintained Zoo in India. And also visit Karanji Lake, a picturesque lake located at the footsteps of Chamundi Hills. Later drive to Srirangapatna were you will visit Ranganathittu Bird Sanctuary, Sri Ranganatha Swamy Temple, Tipu's Summer Palace, Srirangapatna Fort, Gumbuz and Nimishamba Temple. Later at night transfer to Mysore Railway Station / Bus Stand. Depart from Mysore to onward destination.

Three Day - Ooty Conoor Tour from Mysuru

  • Ooty - Coonoor Tour Package 3 Days from Mysore

    Day 1: Mysore to Ooty /Sightseeing

    Meet on arrival at Mysore railway station / bus stand and proceed to Ooty. En-route pass by Bandipur National forest, visit Pykara dam & house boat. On arrival to Ooty check into hotel. After lunch go for Ooty sightseeing Rose Garden, Ooty lake & Botanical Garden. Overnight stay at Ooty.

  • Day 2: Ooty & Coonoor Sightseeing

    After breakfast, proceed to Coonoor by Toy Train. Cab will reach by road and take you for Coonoor Sightseeing - Sim’s Park, Lam’s Rock, Dolphin Nose, Ketty Valley, Tea factory and Doddabetta Peak. Overnight stay at Ooty.
  • Day 3: Departure

    After breakfast, check out hotel, proceed Mysore. Visit 6th & 9th Mile (Shooting Spots). En-route pass by Nanjangud Nanjundeshwara Temple. Proceed back to Mysore and transfer to Mysore Railway Station / Bus Stand. Depart from Mysore to onward destination.
